BTS has many “ First Korean Artists to … ’’ titles. For the past decades of the K-pop industry, K-pop was an Asian thing. Until BTS came along, BTS rose to the top from a bunch of young boys following their dream to winning 18 daesangs.

charts

  • BTS is the first Asian Artist to top Billboard 200.
  • BTS is the first Korean Boy Group to chart on Billboard 100
  • BTS is the Highest Charting K-pop group on the Billboard 100
  • BTS is the was the first Korean artists to be the Top 40 on the Billboard 200
  • Love Yourself: Her was the best selling album on the Goan charts for its sales for its monthly sales with 1.2 Million within the first month of release
  • Love Yourself: Answer was the best selling album on the Goan charts for its sales for its monthly sales with 1.9 Million within the first month of release
  • BTS’s Mic Drop (Steve Aoki Remix) was the first top 40 entry for a K-pop group on Billboard 100
  • BTS is the First Non-Japanese Artist to Top the Japanese Oricon Charts
  • The first pop act to top the Billboard 100 charts twice in one year
  • First K-pop Act to top the Canadian Charts

    Award Shows:

  • BTS was the first K-pop group to perform at the American Music Awards Awards:
  • First Artists being able to win “Artists of the Year” at MAMA Awards twice in a row
  • First K-pop group outside the “Big 3” Companies winning a “daesang” at the Golden Disc Awards and Seoul Music Awards
  • First K-pop Group nominated 3 times in a row for the Billboard Music Awards
  • First K-pop Group winning twice in a row at the Billboard music awards
